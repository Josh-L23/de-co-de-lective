Our Story

From Classroom to Code

We began as five students with a shared passion for technology and innovation. Meeting during our first year of university, we quickly discovered our synergy as critical thinkers and problem solvers. What started as a study group evolved into a dynamic team eager to explore the world of programming.

After mastering Java fundamentals, we dove headfirst into JavaScript and web development. Embracing the "learn by doing" philosophy, we created our first project - The StudentPlanner, a comprehensive web application designed to help students organize their academic lives.

Today, we're The Code Collective - a team of four Bachelor of Science in Information Systems students and one Bachelor of Education in Computer Science student. We combine technical expertise with a passion for creating meaningful educational tools.
